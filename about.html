Project Description

Data & Variables

The project will use a de-identified, publicly available hospital level dataset along with logit regression modeling and survey methodology to answer the research question. The main independent variable physician leadership in a hospital (Likert scale). The five dependent variables (one outcome per model, so five models total): ED care integration, hospital EHR integration, shared-decision making with patients, challenges towards adapting new evidence-based clinical treatments, and challenges towards adapting new evidence-based care delivery innovations. Control variables include type of hospital (academic, etc.), presence of alternative payment contracts, and percentage of revenues tied into quality metrics.

The project will also allow for the creation of interesting visualizations of hospital-level characteristics. What percentage of hospitals are academic-affiliated or owned? What percentage of hospitals report being part of an alternative payment model contract? These visualizations will help provide important context about the type of hospitals in the dataset. It may be of interest to also make a table of summary statistics for each variable and a variable descriptions table to get a better sense of which variables are available for use. All analysis will be conducted in R.
